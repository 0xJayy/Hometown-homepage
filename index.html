Visit El Salvador

Enjoy the delicious food, natural attractions and welcoming people of El Salvador.

Top three things to do in El Salvador

Go to the beach to surf and get some much needed vitamin D.

The beaches in El Salvador are a popular place to have fun and enjoy the waves.

Hike a mountain and enjoy the altitude

The mountains and volcanos are a great way to get some fresh air and see breathtaking views.

Go to Coatepeque lake to relax and enjoy life.

The lake of Coatepeque offers delicious food, being able to take a swim, and get on a jet ski.
